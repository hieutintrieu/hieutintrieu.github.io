Giới Thiệu Bản Thân

We have to really think about what we dare to pay for that passion of ours. Money, health, time, fun, accepting away from family or breaking up with a lover ... Do we dare trade off for the reality of our passion? Everything has its price. If we are just passionate and do not dare to trade off, there is not enough passion.
